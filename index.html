Calligritype is a community of over 350,000 artists and designers that come together to share and appreciate beautiful, well-crafted, curated work.

About Us

Calligritype was started in 2013 by Dennis Cortés to collect inspiration. Over time, people began to recognize the usefulness and careful curation behind this idea. Calligritype has since become one of the largest and best places to find resources and inspiration for lettering, calligraphy, and typography. We pride ourselves on our integrity and innovation in a crowded industry.
